About me

I have been working for an international publisher for over two decades. Some of my friends may ask me if it's too stable to pursue my ambitions or dreams. In fact, I have been working hard to develop my second career during these years. I graduated from Animation Mentor and iAnimate in 2007 and 2012. I also enrolled the master class of GOBELINS, l'école de l'image and Anomalia in 2009 and 2016. As a freelance character animator, I am participating in some projects of TV series and game production.

To me, character animation is amazing. It helps me to reveal the nature of human beings. At some moments, you really feel that you are going to make something alive.
